Trunk

So you decided to check for something useful you could have in the trunk. In there, you find three different things :


-The first one is a cinder block, you had no idea why the hell there was one in your trunk but it might eventually be useful.

-The second object is a PC, your forever friend.

-The third one is the drone you made yourself for a school project. Very cool item.


Witch one of those three items will you pick? Yes one, you don't want to hurt yourself carrying to much things at a time.

Choose wisely...
